DHOOM Instructions

In this game the thief robs a bank and runs away.
You have to help the police to catch the thief by giving the correct answers. The question will appear on the screen with the answer, this answer may or may not be correct.

If the answer is correct then you have to click on the ( ✔ ) correct button and if the answer is wrong then you have to click on ( X ) wrong button.
